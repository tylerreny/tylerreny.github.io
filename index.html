Tyler is a postdoctoral research fellow with the SURGE Lab at Washington University St. Louis. He received his PhD in political science from UCLA. His work examines the causes and political consequences of identity politics and has been published in American Political Science Review, British Journal of Political Science, Public Opinion Quarterly, Comparative Political Studies, Political Psychology, Political Research Quarterly, Politics and Gender, Politics, Group, and Identities, and others. He has also published in popular outlets including the Los Angeles Times and the Washington Post.

Tyler is also the project coordinator for the Nationscape Survey (Democracy Fund + UCLA) and Co-PI on the ongoing Multi-Wave National Panel Study of COVID-19.
